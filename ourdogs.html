BIO

Ellie is a two year old, AKC registered light Golden Retriever. She has a gorgeous thin, wavy coat and weighs about 60 pounds. Ellie loves to hike mountain trails and is always ready for an adventure. She is very receptive to what we ask of her and emotionally intuitive. She loves her toys, is always ready to snuggle, and wants you to hold her paw whenever possible. Ellie is silly, sweet and will be such a wonderful mother.
